Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit Elias Musleh's portfolio website (the "Site").

Personal Information We Collect

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device.

How We Use Your Personal Information

We use the information that we collect generally to:

  • Maintain and improve the Site
  • Analyze how visitors use the Site
  • Monitor and prevent any problems with the Site

Data Retention

When you submit information through the contact form, we maintain that information for our records unless you request that we delete this information.

Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can request deletion of your personal data by:

  1. Sending an email to contact@eliasmusleh.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Including in your email:
    • Your full name
    • Email address used when contacting us
    • Brief description of your interactions with our website

Data Deletion Process

Once we receive your request:

  1. We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours
  2. We will verify your identity to protect your privacy
  3. We will process your deletion request within 30 days
  4. We will send you confirmation once your data has been deleted

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
